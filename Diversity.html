Equality Diversity

Diversity- This relates to the differences between people. This could be in reference to gender, race, ability, social, cultural or ethnic background in addition to individual characteristics. The United Kingdom has a very diverse population, with people from a wide range of nationalities who have different abilities, speak a variety of languages and have distinctive cultural traditions. It is very important for professionals in the early years sector to develop . This means recognising, respecting and valuing differences in order to meet the individual needs of children and their families.
