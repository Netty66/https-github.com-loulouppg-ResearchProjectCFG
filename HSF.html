The Food Safety and Hygiene (England) Regulations 2013 Provides guidance on the preparation, storage and cooking of food, as well as the requirements for staff training in food hygiene Control of Substances Hazardous to Health (COSHH) 2002 Outlines regulations on the storage and usage of dangerous chemicals (including cleaning substances and medicines) Reporting of Injuries, Diseases and Dangerous Occurrences Regulations (RIDDOR) 2013 Outlines the procedures for reporting accidents, injuries and infectious diseases such as Tuberculosis (TB) and meningitis to the Health and Safety Executive (HSE) The safeguarding and welfare requirements of the Statutory Framework for the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS 2014) outline the health and safety policies and procedures that apply to early years settings.
